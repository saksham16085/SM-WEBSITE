A Poem

MY GRANDMOTHER


"Sitting by the window, I was staring at the horizon,
The tears in my eyes had no reason.
I sat there deep in thought,
They were streaming down, but I fought.
My heartbeat was rapid, my fists were clenched
I knew my happiness was at an end.
The light went out and yet I could see,
A face through the stars, smiling at me.
I knew that face was of my fairy godmother,
No one else but my Grandmother.
She smiled at me through her warm eyes,
She was far away grinning wide.
Those joyful memories flashed through my mind,
I got up leaving my dread behind.
Those precious moments with my parents, family and friends
and then I knew this is not how this ends.
Now, I had the strength to face it,
Because she taught me- “LIFE IS WHAT YOU MAKE IT !!””
